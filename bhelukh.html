BHULEKH

Online Access

Bhulekh provides online access to land records, making it easy for landowners to access their records from anywhere in the country. The platform is available 24/7, and landowners can access their records by simply entering their details on the platform.

Real-time Updates

Bhulekh provides real-time updates and changes to land records. This means that any changes or updates made to the records are reflected immediately, reducing the chances of errors and inaccuracies.

Transparency

Bhulekh is designed to be transparent, with all changes and updates being tracked and recorded. This makes it difficult for officials to make fraudulent changes to land records, as all changes are monitored and tracked.

User-friendly Interface

Bhulekh is designed to be a user-friendly platform, with a simple and easy-to-use interface. Landowners can access their records with just a few clicks, making it a convenient platform for maintaining land records.

Easy Access

Bhulekh provides easy access to land records, making it convenient for landowners to access their records from anywhere in the country. This reduces the need for middlemen and brokers, making the process of maintaining land records more transparent and efficient.
